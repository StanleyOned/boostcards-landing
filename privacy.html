Privacy Policy

How BoostCards protects your data and privacy

Last updated: August 30, 2025

Information We Collect

BoostCards collects minimal information to provide our AI-powered flashcard service:

  • Study Content: Text, images, PDFs, and voice recordings you upload to generate flashcards
  • Usage Data: Study sessions, progress tracking, and app analytics to improve your learning experience
  • Account Information: Email address and subscription status for app access and billing
  • Device Data: iOS version and device type for app optimization

How We Use Your Information

  • AI Processing: Generate personalized flashcards from your study materials
  • Sync & Backup: CloudKit integration to sync your flashcards across Apple devices
  • Learning Analytics: Track your progress and optimize spaced repetition algorithms
  • App Improvement: Analyze usage patterns to enhance features and performance

Data Storage & Security

Your data security is our priority:

  • Apple CloudKit: Your flashcards are stored securely in Apple's CloudKit with end-to-end encryption
  • AI Processing: Content sent to OpenAI for flashcard generation is processed securely and not stored permanently
  • Local Storage: Voice recordings and images are processed locally when possible
  • No Third-Party Sharing: We never sell or share your personal study content with third parties

Voice & Image Processing

  • Speech Recognition: Uses Apple's on-device Speech framework when possible
  • Image Processing: Apple's Vision framework processes images locally on your device
  • PDF Text Extraction: Performed locally using Apple's frameworks
  • Temporary Processing: Audio recordings are temporarily processed for text conversion, then deleted

Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • Delete Data: Remove individual flashcards or entire study sets anytime
  • Export Data: Request a copy of your study materials
  • Account Deletion: Delete your account and all associated data permanently
  • Opt-Out: Disable analytics or AI features in app settings

Third-Party Services

BoostCards integrates with these trusted services:

  • OpenAI: Powers AI flashcard generation (content processed securely, not stored)
  • Apple CloudKit: Secure data sync across your Apple devices
  • App Store: Handles subscription billing and management

Children's Privacy

BoostCards is safe for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. Parents can review and delete their child's data at any time.

Contact Us

For privacy questions or data requests:

  • Email: privacy@boostcards.app
  • In-App: Use the contact form in BoostCards settings

Updates to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We'll notify users of significant changes through the app or email.
